Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage your fashion retail inventory efficiently

Real-Time Monitoring

Track inventory levels across all your stores in real-time. Get instant visibility into stock movements and trends.

Smart Notifications

Automated low-stock alerts via AWS SNS ensure you never miss a restock opportunity. Stay ahead of demand.

Role-Based Access

Secure IAM-style access control for Admins, Store Managers, and Suppliers. Everyone sees what they need.

Shipment Tracking

End-to-end visibility of shipments from supplier to store. Track status, confirm deliveries, and update inventory automatically.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboards with inventory trends, shipment analytics, and performance metrics for data-driven decisions.

CSV Export

Export products, shipments, and user data to CSV for reporting, analysis, or integration with other systems.
